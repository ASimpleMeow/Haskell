STV

Stage 1:
only first picks are counted

Stage 2:
Any votes recevied for a candidate above the quota are transfered to the next choice.
If no one else reaches the quota, candidate with lowest votes is eliminated

Stage 3:
All of the eliminated candidates votes (from last stage) are transfered to other candidates
Repeat until all seats for election are filled
